Elle Maraccini

Hi!I am working artist in the San Francisco, Bay Area. I am a high school senior who prides herself in professionalism and working with businesses to create amazing products. Whether it is designing pins for the FOX show The Orville or making a logo for my history teacher, I am always ready to adapt and work with people to translate their fantasies into reality. I hope to go into children's books and I have already written and illustrated two tales of my own. I have learned so much in 18 years but I want to learn so much more
